Design decisions

Register

Wellfound + Hired reference register: confident, information-dense, professional. Not student-platform soft, not enterprise-corporate stiff. Navy dominates; amber appears at most 3 times per screen (badges, featured indicators, CTA accents).

Typography

Geist Sans (NOT Inter) throughout, Geist Mono for all numeric data — salary ranges, job counts, timestamps. Salary always appears in monospace so it reads like a number, not a label.

120% moments

Each screen has one detail pushed to 120%: v1 has filter active states + featured ribbon; v3 has the split preview pane; job-detail has the navy apply card with salary in large mono; post-a-job has the live preview panel; company-profile has the geometric stats in the hero.

Anti-slop applied

No purple/blue gradients. No SVG-drawn humans (picsum for future photo integration). No rounded card + left-border-accent pattern. No "Acme Corp" — Folio Labs, Caelum Capital, Pith, Marrow Studios, Vexor Rail. No emoji icons — Phosphor-style SVGs only.
