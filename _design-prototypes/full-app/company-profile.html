About

Folio Labs builds the infrastructure layer that creative teams depend on to work together at scale. Our products are used by design-led organisations across media, architecture, and gaming — spanning teams from three people in a studio to hundreds across a global enterprise.

We are a remote-first engineering organisation with an optional office in Shoreditch. Our platform handles hundreds of millions of operations daily, and we hold ourselves to high standards of reliability and performance. The engineering team is small, senior, and deeply collaborative. We write a lot, move deliberately, and ship things that last.

We are a company that measures its success by the depth of trust our customers place in our infrastructure — not by vanity metrics or growth-at-any-cost.
