About Folio Labs

Folio Labs builds infrastructure for institutional capital markets. Our platform handles over £2.1B in daily transactions across 14 currencies for 340+ fund managers — from emerging market hedge funds to sovereign wealth vehicles. Founded in 2019, we're Series B (£38M, led by Balderton) and growing to 180 people across London and New York.

We believe financial infrastructure should be invisible — reliable to the point of being boring, performant to the point of being forgettable. The platform team is where that belief gets implemented.

The role

We're looking for a Senior Platform Engineer to own the reliability, scalability, and developer experience of Folio's core services. This is a senior IC role with genuine technical authority — you'll set direction for how our systems observe, scale, and recover from failure.

You'll be embedded in the Platform tribe (5 engineers), working directly with the CTO and squad leads across the product surface. There is a clear path to Staff Engineer for the right person.

What you'll own

  • Operate and evolve our Kubernetes clusters (EKS) running 140+ microservices across three AWS regions (eu-west-1, us-east-1, ap-southeast-1)
  • Drive our observability maturity: Prometheus + Thanos, Grafana dashboards, distributed tracing via Jaeger, structured logging with Loki
  • Build internal developer tooling — golden path CI/CD, service scaffolding, database provisioning automation — that removes toil for 38 product engineers
  • Own incident response posture: runbooks, on-call rotation design, postmortem facilitation, and systematic reliability improvements
  • Partner with our security team on SOC 2 Type II certification (target: Q3 2026) — particularly around infrastructure controls, audit logging, and access management
  • Contribute meaningfully to our platform roadmap — we expect senior engineers to generate ideas, not just execute on them

What we're looking for

  • 6+ years of production platform engineering or SRE experience — ideally in a fintech or high-stakes transactional context
  • Strong Go for tooling and services; Python is also welcome for automation
  • Deep AWS knowledge that goes beyond console familiarity: EKS internals, RDS multi-AZ, SQS dead-letter patterns, CloudFront behaviours
  • You've designed and operated an on-call rotation — and measurably improved it after joining
  • Comfortable with the ambiguity of a Series B: priorities shift, documentation lags, and you build the thing that makes both better

Nice to have

  • Experience with financial systems: FIX protocol, settlement flows, or regulatory reporting infrastructure
  • Familiarity with Crossplane or other infrastructure-as-code platforms at scale
  • Prior experience driving a DORA metrics improvement programme

What we offer

  • £72,000 – £95,000 base depending on experience, plus equity (0.1–0.25% on standard 4yr cliff/vest)
  • 25 days holiday + bank holidays + 1 additional day per year of tenure (up to 30)
  • Private health insurance via Vitality (dental and optical included)
  • £2,000 per year learning budget, no approval required for items under £500
  • Hybrid: 3 days in our Shoreditch office, 2 from home — flexible on which days
  • Enhanced parental leave: 26 weeks full pay for primary carer, 12 weeks for secondary
