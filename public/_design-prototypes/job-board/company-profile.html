About Folio Labs

Folio Labs was founded in 2019 by a team of ex-Goldman engineers who believed the infrastructure underpinning capital markets was decades overdue for a rebuild. Most fund managers still process settlements via spreadsheets, FTP servers, and handcrafted custodian integrations. We're changing that.

Our platform handles end-to-end post-trade infrastructure — from transaction capture through settlement, reconciliation, custody, and regulatory reporting — for 340+ fund managers across the UK, US, and Singapore. We process £2.1B daily, across 14 currencies, with a 99.97% uptime SLA.

We're Series B (£38M, led by Balderton Capital) and growing fast. Our engineering culture is high-trust, high-ownership, and deeply opposed to busywork. We ship monthly, run quarterly planning, and measure success by outcomes not output.
