About the role

Folio Labs is building the infrastructure that powers capital markets for fund managers. We process over £2.1B in daily transactions across 14 currencies, and our platform team is the backbone that makes this possible.

We're looking for a Senior Platform Engineer who can own the reliability and observability of our core systems — someone who's done this before, who writes the runbook and then deletes it once the system is self-healing.

What you'll own

  • Design and operate Kubernetes clusters running 140+ microservices across three AWS regions
  • Drive our observability maturity: Prometheus, Grafana, distributed tracing with Jaeger
  • Build internal tooling that removes friction for 30+ engineers — golden paths, not guardrails
  • Partner with security to achieve SOC 2 Type II by Q3 2026
  • Lead incident response and postmortem culture — blameless, systemic

What we're looking for

  • 6+ years of production platform or SRE experience
  • Strong Go or Python for tooling and automation
  • Deep AWS knowledge (EKS, RDS, SQS, CloudFront) — not just certifications
  • You've led an on-call rotation and improved it
