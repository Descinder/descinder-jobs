You upload up to 3 base CVs (PDF, DOCX, or text). When you want to apply to a native role, you pick one base CV and one target job — or paste the job description manually. The AI (Groq's Llama 3 model, with Claude Haiku as a fallback) reads both and returns a rewritten version that emphasises the skills and experience most relevant to that specific role. You see the output, can edit it, then export as PDF or copy to clipboard. Up to 30 generations per calendar month — enough for an active search. Your base CVs are never changed.
